About.

1. About me

London based User Experience Designer with 4 years of commercial experience in, among others, e-commerce, data-driven interfaces and online media.

Experienced with the whole product development cycle. Clarify and report business and client requirements, validate ideas with wireframes and prototypes, research with quantitative and qualitative analysis for reasoning support, develop pixel-perfect mockups, UI guidelines, Visual Design, HTML/CSS/JS, and Google Analytics.

2. Approach on user experience

I believe that user experience design is more than the look and feel of a product. While often imperceptible, it carries an intrinsic background strategy with great value that promotes growth by distinguishing brands from the competition, attract new clients while retaining old ones, prevent expensive development mistakes, increase conversions among others. It has been statically proven that companies who value design outperform their rivals significantly.

Design-driven companies outperform the S&P 500 by 211%

Source

Product interfaces are one of the most significant touchpoints between businesses and its customers. It's essential to handle its development and implementation with ease and discernment before delivering it to the end-user. As a User Experience designer, my work is to perform an extensive analysis to product interfaces and combine its results into seamless, visually appealing and easy to use product interfaces.

3. My persona

I am passionate about technology, business, and design in general. My background education is in Product Design (BA) and HCI (MA), and I have been playing with computers since the times when the internet speed was 56kb/s, images took two minutes to load and mIRC was still a thing.

I also enjoy telling stories through documentaries, making sounds with synthesizers, and have a small photography studio which I use as part of my work.
